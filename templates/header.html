What Imagify does?
  • Lets you upload image
  • Predicts what the image is all about
  • Gives you multiple probability what the image could be...
  • Helps you learn by taking the picture of things you do not know

HowTos, Overview and References.
  • Imagify is build using Keras(Highlevel API), Backend Tensorflow, Python, Flask, and other minor dependencies
  • I borrowed the code logics from https://blog.keras.io
  • I referred to several tutorials on https://python.org
  • I referred to several Stackoverflow QA
